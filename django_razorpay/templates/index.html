About Us

Project Meera, the brainchild of Enactus Thapar, intends to strike out the odds that come in the way of building a healthy ecosystem and save oceans from the depletion of Coral Reefs. We plan on taking initiative in generating a sustainable, positive impact on upcoming ocean challenges and long-term reforms by bringing this grave issue to public attention and preserving these beautiful creatures.

Collaborations

Coastal Impact

Coastal impact is an NGO in Goa that studies and monitors the marine ecosystems, supports awareness, and research regarding coral reefs and promotes their conservation and sustenance. Not only that, but they also help build interest and engagement amongst local communities and visitors about the extraordinary marine ecosystems of India. Project Meera has collaborated with Coastal Impact to seek their assistance in facilitating coral adoption.

Futureseed Earth Foundation

Futureseed earth foundation (FSEF) is an NGO that provides services in many aspects of the field of work such as Environment, Agriculture, etc. It majorly deals with the field of environmental education and awareness programs. They as a team striving forward to not only protect the environment but also to re-establish the habitat for the animals that are being snatched from them. Project Meera has collaborated with Futureseed Earth Foundation to seek their assistance in fund generation.

With You

WITH YOU Organisation is an embodiment of “Donate Red, Spread Green and Save Blue”. It is a youth-driven, non-profit organization working for the betterment of society in various aspects. Registered as an NGO in 2015, With You is a team of 300+ active social workers who will collaborate with us in our efforts to protect the coral reefs through coral replantation.
